### 1. AI 에이전트 기반 자율 쇼핑 시스템의 등장

**핵심 포인트:**
- 구글 Gemini가 픽셀 폰에서 사용자 대신 식료품 주문과 승차 예약 수행
- Perplexity AI 에이전트의 아마존 쇼핑 기능이 법정 분쟁으로 중단
- B2B/B2C 기업들의 에이전틱 커머스 투자 확산

**D2C 시사점:**
AI 에이전트가 실제 구매를 대행하는 시대가 도래하면서, D2C 브랜드는 음성/텍스트 기반 주문 시스템과 AI 최적화된 쇼핑 경험 개발이 필수가 되었습니다.

D2C 브랜드는 AI 기반 반품 사기에 대비한 정교한 탐지 시스템 구축과 AI 도구 사용 시 윤리적 가이드라인 강화가 시급합니다.

## Key Report Summaries

<div class="report-card">

### 1. Emergence of AI Agent-Based Autonomous Shopping Systems

**Key Takeaways:**
- Google Gemini performs grocery ordering and ride booking on behalf of users on Pixel phones
- Perplexity's Amazon shopping AI agent suspended due to legal disputes
- B2B/B2C companies increasing agentic commerce investments

**Implications for D2C:**
As AI agents begin making actual purchases, D2C brands must develop voice/text-based ordering systems and AI-optimized shopping experiences to remain competitive.

📄 **Source:** [Google's latest Pixel drop allows Gemini to order groceries for you and more](https://www.theverge.com/tech/888295/google-gemini-pixel-drop-march-2026) | [Judge orders Perplexity to stop AI agents from shopping on Amazon](https://www.theverge.com/ai-artificial-intelligence/892401/amazon-perplexity-ai-shopping-agent-court-order)

### 2. Evolution of AI-Based Fraud and Security Threats

**Key Takeaways:**
- Brands like Boll & Branch and Bogg experiencing surge in return fraud using AI-generated fake photos and receipts
- Grammarly facing class-action lawsuit over AI feature that cloned expert identities
- AI-generated content confirmed to have no copyright protection

**Implications for D2C:**
D2C brands urgently need sophisticated fraud detection systems for AI-based return fraud and must strengthen ethical guidelines when using AI tools.

📄 **Source:** [From Boll & Branch to Bogg, brands battle a surge of AI-driven return fraud](https://digiday.com/marketing/from-boll-branch-to-bogg-brands-battle-a-surge-of-ai-driven-return-fraud/?utm_campaign=digidaydis&utm_medium=rss&utm_source=general-rss) | [One of Grammarly's 'experts' is suing the company over its identity-stealing AI feature](https://www.theverge.com/ai-artificial-intelligence/893451/grammarly-ai-lawsuit-julia-angwin)

### 3. Success Story of Direct Manufacturer-to-Consumer Model

**Key Takeaways:**
- Quince achieves $10B valuation with $500M funding round
- Direct manufacturer-to-consumer connection enabling 'affordable luxury' positioning
- Annual revenue exceeding $1B with triple-digit growth rates

**Implications for D2C:**
Direct manufacturer connections and reasonably-priced premium product strategies prove to be key drivers for rapid D2C growth and high valuations.

## 🌏 Regional Observations

**AMER (Americas):**
The US is establishing legal precedents for AI agent shopping and regulatory frameworks, requiring AMER D2C companies to prioritize compliance infrastructure. Google's settlement with Epic also reduces app store commission burden.

**EMEA (Europe, Middle East, Africa):**
EMEA faces strengthened AI ethics and data protection regulations, with cases like Grammarly's identity cloning expected to trigger particularly sensitive responses, necessitating stricter AI governance frameworks.

**APAC (Asia Pacific):**
APAC stands to benefit most from direct manufacturer connection models and physical AI manufacturing technologies, with strategies like Quince's manufacturer-to-consumer approach being particularly effective in this region.
