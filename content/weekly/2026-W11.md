### 3. 옴니채널 전략과 물리적 매장의 재정의

Target이 2,000번째 매장을 오픈하며 '향상된 고객 경험의 미래'를 선보이고 올해 30개 이상 신규 매장 계획을 발표했습니다. Petco는 매출 감소 상황에서도 '통합 옴니채널' 경험 구축에 집중하고 있으며, Ross는 17개 신규 매장을 열며 적극적인 오프라인 확장을 지속하고 있습니다. 이는 디지털 시대에도 물리적 접점의 가치가 재평가되고 있음을 보여줍니다.

### 1. Legal Regulation of AI Shopping Agents Intensifies

A federal court issued an injunction blocking Perplexity's AI agents from shopping on Amazon on behalf of users. Amazon sued Perplexity in November for unauthorized account access, with the court finding strong evidence of improper authorization. Meanwhile, Amazon is expanding its own AI-powered 'Buy for Me' and 'Shop Direct' tools to offer new options for third-party merchants.

**Sources:**
- [Judge orders Perplexity to stop AI agents from shopping on Amazon](https://www.theverge.com/ai-artificial-intelligence/892401/amazon-perplexity-ai-shopping-agent-court-order)
- [Amazon opens up new AI-enabled Buy for Me, Shop Direct options for merchants](https://www.digitalcommerce360.com/2026/03/11/amazon-opens-up-new-ai-enabled-buy-for-me-shop-direct-options-for-merchants/)

**💡 So What for D2C:** D2C companies must secure clear user consent and platform approval when implementing AI agents to avoid legal risks while exploring new sales opportunities through Amazon's AI tools.

### 2. AI Companies Face Ethics Controversies and Trust Issues

Grammarly faces a class-action lawsuit over its 'Expert Review' AI feature that cloned real experts without permission. Journalist Julia Angwin sued for unauthorized identity use, leading Grammarly to discontinue the feature. Conversely, Anthropic's ethical stance in refusing Pentagon contracts has yielded positive results in brand trust, downloads, and enterprise deals.

**Sources:**
- [One of Grammarly's 'experts' is suing the company over its identity-stealing AI feature](https://www.theverge.com/ai-artificial-intelligence/893451/grammarly-ai-lawsuit-julia-angwin)

**💡 So What for D2C:** Ethical considerations and user consent in AI development directly impact brand trust, making transparent and value-aligned AI partner selection crucial for long-term success.

This demonstrates the renewed value of physical touchpoints in the digital era.

**Sources:**

### 4. Agentic Commerce Faces Reality Check and Adjustments

Agentic commerce is facing practical challenges in B2B ecommerce as initial visions of AI agents automatically making purchases encounter implementation difficulties. Major companies like OpenAI and Amazon are adjusting their strategies. While a survey of 600 ecommerce companies shows increased AI investment in customer experience, pricing, and inventory management, gradual implementation is proving more practical than full automation.

**Sources:**
- [Agentic commerce faces reality check in B2B ecommerce](https://www.digitalcommerce360.com/2026/03/10/agentic-commerce-faces-reality-check-in-b2b-ecommerce/)
- [B2B and B2C companies increase AI investment as agentic commerce gains traction](https://www.digitalcommerce360.com/2026/03/13/b2b-b2c-ai-investment-agentic-commerce-traction/)

**💡 So What for D2C:** D2C companies should adopt gradual AI integration strategies prioritizing customer experience over rushed full automation when implementing agentic commerce solutions.

Middle East conflicts are disrupting global shipping networks and raising air freight costs. The strategic importance of the Strait of Hormuz, handling 20% of global energy trade, affects data center and electricity costs. Meanwhile, CBP is developing a 4-step system for tariff refund processing as follow-up measures to trade policy changes.

**Sources:**
- [War escalation raises shipping costs and delivery risks for B2B sellers](https://www.digitalcommerce360.com/2026/03/13/war-escalation-raises-shipping-costs-and-delivery-risks-for-b2b-sellers/)

## 📈 This Week's Observations: Risks & Opportunities

1. **Risk — Legal Risks in AI Shopping Agents**: Increasing litigation and service blocking risks due to unauthorized account access require establishing clear authorization frameworks.

2. **Risk — Rising Operational Costs from Geopolitical Conflicts**: Middle East conflicts are driving up shipping and energy costs, creating margin pressure and customer price transfer challenges for D2C brands.

3. **Opportunity — AI-Powered Personalization Service Expansion**: Following Stitch Fix's success with AI tools, opportunities for revenue recovery and customer retention improvement through AI personalization are growing.

4. **Opportunity — Differentiation Value of Ethical AI Partnerships**: Like Anthropic's case, collaborating with ethically-aligned AI partners positively impacts brand trust and enterprise customer acquisition.

5. **Observation — Gradual Evolution of Agentic Commerce**: Gradual AI integration prioritizing customer experience over full automation is emerging as a more practical approach than completely automated AI shopping.
