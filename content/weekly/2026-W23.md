### 3. 프리미엄 제품 전략이 경제적 압박 속에서도 성장 동력으로 작용

울타 뷰티가 1분기 매출 11% 성장을 기록하며 고급 미용제품 전략의 성공을 입증했습니다. 소비자들의 지출 압박에도 불구하고 프리미엄 상품군이 성장을 견인했으며, 회사는 뉴욕에 체험형 매장 오픈을 계획하고 있습니다. 반면 Dollar General의 매출이 3.4% 급증한 것은 고객들이 가속화된 속도로 저가 제품으로 갈아타고 있기 때문입니다.

Amazon opened its AI agent technology to external retailers starting with Kate Spade partnerships, while Salesforce acquired Contentful to strengthen Agentforce's content layer. Google is expanding Universal Cart to Walmart and Target, accelerating AI shopping cart platform development. Ulta Beauty shared early AI assistant results showing ecommerce growth significantly outpacing physical stores.

**Sources:**
- [Amazon offers AI agent tech to other retailers](https://www.retaildive.com/news/amazon-to-offer-ai-agents-to-other-retailers/821597/)

<div class="trend-box">

### 2. Answer Engine Optimization Emerges as Essential Marketing Strategy

HubSpot's 2026 AEO report found AI search was the top predictor of purchase intent for CRM software buyers. AI search behavior may cause traffic dips but delivers higher-quality leads, and websites flagged as Google's 'Preferred Sources' are gaining increased visibility in AI search results. Industry experts warn that treating generative search as just an extension of SEO could cost billions in losses.

**Sources:**
- [AI search behavior: What it means for your marketing strategy in 2026](https://blog.hubspot.com/marketing/ai-search-behavior)

5. **Observation — Shift Toward Entertainment-Centric Marketing**: Fundamental paradigm change observed as brands prioritize entertainment value over direct advertising in their marketing strategies
