## 🏆 This Week's Top 5 Trends

<div class="trend-box">

### 1. Explosive Growth of AI Agent-Based Commerce

US agentic commerce sales are projected to reach $1 trillion by 2030, with HubSpot positioning itself as an 'agentic customer platform.' Wayfair has partnered with Meta, Google, and Pinterest to develop advertising through AI agents. DoorDash has introduced AI tools for merchant onboarding and content optimization.

**Sources:**
- [US agentic sales to reach $1T by 2030: report](https://www.paymentsdive.com/news/agentic-commerce-us-one-trillion-2030/819490/)
- [Our Vision for Building an Open Ecosystem for the Agent Era](https://blog.hubspot.com/marketing/our-vision-for-building-an-open-ecosystem-for-the-agent-era)

**💡 So What for D2C:** LG D2C must build AI agent-based customer service and sales automation systems to respond to the rapidly growing agentic commerce market.

</div>

<div class="trend-box">

### 2. Accelerating Retail Industry Platform Consolidation

GameStop submitted a $56 billion acquisition proposal for eBay, attempting to create a legitimate competitor to Amazon. This signals platform consolidation and competitive landscape reshaping in retail. Walmart and Costco are positioned to emerge as winners despite unfavorable retail environments.

**Sources:**

### 3. AI Personalization Trust Crisis and Need for New Approaches

Consumer AI usage is increasing while fatigue and skepticism are also growing. Personalization fails when it feels like surveillance, and most AI personalization strategies fail during execution. Trust-building has emerged as a key element for successful personalization.

**Sources:**
- [AI use and fatigue growing among consumers](https://martech.org/ai-use-and-fatigue-growing-among-consumers/)

1. **Risk — AI Fatigue and Declining Trust**: Despite increasing AI usage, growing consumer fatigue and skepticism may limit the effectiveness of AI-powered marketing tools

2. **Opportunity — Agentic Commerce Early Adoption**: Opportunity to gain competitive advantage in the agentic commerce market projected to reach $1 trillion by 2030 through AI agent-based customer experience automation

3. **Risk — Increasing Platform Dependency Risk**: Large platform consolidations and M&A activities like GameStop-eBay bid may increase platform dependency risks for D2C brands

4. **Opportunity — Generative AI Search Optimization**: Brand visibility in AI-generated responses becomes a new marketing battleground, offering competitive advantages through GEO strategy implementation

5. **Observation — Creator Marketing Evolution**: Memes and social content are evolving into serious marketing strategies, making cultural trend-based brand communication increasingly important
