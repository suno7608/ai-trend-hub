Scrunch는 AI 생성 답변에서 브랜드 노출을 전문적으로 모니터링하는 AEO(Answer Engine Optimization) 특화 도구인 반면, Semrush는 기존 SEO 기능에 AI 가시성 툴킷을 추가한 종합 플랫폼이다. 두 도구의 선택은 AI 답변 최적화에 집중할 것인지, 아니면 키워드·순위·백링크를 포괄하는 통합 SEO 관리가 필요한지에 따라 달라진다. AI 검색 비중이 높아지면서 브랜드의 AI 답변 내 등장 여부가 디지털 마케팅의 새로운 핵심 지표로 부상하고 있다.

</div>
<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

Scrunch is a specialized AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) tool designed to monitor how a brand appears in AI-generated answers, while Semrush is a comprehensive SEO platform that has added an AI Visibility Toolkit to its existing suite. The choice between the two depends on whether marketers need a dedicated AI visibility solution or an all-in-one SEO stack. As AI-driven search grows in prominence, brand visibility within AI-generated answers is becoming a critical new marketing metric.

</div>

## Key Points

- Scrunch specializes in AEO, tracking brand presence in AI-generated answers from tools like ChatGPT and Perplexity
- Semrush added an AI Visibility Toolkit to its established SEO platform, offering a unified but broader solution
- The rise of AI search is creating a new optimization discipline distinct from traditional SEO
