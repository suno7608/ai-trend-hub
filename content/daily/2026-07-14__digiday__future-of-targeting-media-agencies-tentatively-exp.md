미디어 에이전시들이 AI 기반 광고 집행의 새로운 방식으로 벡터 기반 타겟팅을 실험적으로 탐색하고 있다. 벡터 기반 플래닝은 전통적인 인구통계나 키워드 대신 의미적 유사성을 활용해 더 정밀한 오디언스 타겟팅을 가능하게 한다. 아직 매우 초기 단계이지만, AI 친화적 광고주들에게 미디어 바잉을 개선할 잠재력이 있다고 평가된다.

</div>
<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

Media agencies are tentatively experimenting with vector-based planning as a more AI-forward approach to media buying and audience targeting. Unlike traditional demographic or keyword-based targeting, vector-based methods leverage semantic similarity to identify and reach audiences more precisely. The approach is described as 'very experimental' but holds promise for advertisers seeking to align with AI-driven media environments.

</div>

## Key Points

- Vector-based targeting uses semantic similarity rather than traditional demographics or keywords
- Currently in very experimental stages across media agencies
- Could offer AI-forward advertisers improved precision in media buys
- Represents a structural shift in how audience targeting may be defined in AI-native environments
