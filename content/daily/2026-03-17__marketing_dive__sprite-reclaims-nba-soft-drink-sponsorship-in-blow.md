<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

Sprite has reclaimed the NBA soft drink sponsorship, dealing a blow to PepsiCo's Starry brand. Starry had effectively held this title since its launch in 2023. Sprite has maintained active involvement in basketball culture, including through its 'Obey Your Thirst' campaign.
