<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

Outdoor apparel brand Cotopaxi has expanded its ecommerce strategy by joining online marketplaces in addition to its own website, physical stores, and wholesale channels. According to Chief Global Officer Stephan Jacob, Cotopaxi now sells on three main marketplaces. This reflects a growing trend among brands to pursue multi-channel distribution to maximize online sales reach.
