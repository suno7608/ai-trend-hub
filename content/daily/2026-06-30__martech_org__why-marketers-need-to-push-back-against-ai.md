Research indicates that excessive reliance on AI tools can erode critical thinking skills among users, making independent human judgment more strategically valuable than ever. The article urges marketers to actively push back against AI recommendations and maintain analytical rigor in their decision-making processes. As AI automates more marketing tasks, the differentiating value of human marketers lies in their capacity for critical evaluation and strategic creativity.

- Studies show AI over-reliance can erode critical thinking capabilities in knowledge workers
