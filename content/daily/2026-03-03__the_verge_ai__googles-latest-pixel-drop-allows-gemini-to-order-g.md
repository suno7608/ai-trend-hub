<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

Google has added new features to Pixel phones in its March update, including the ability for its Gemini AI assistant to perform tasks like ordering groceries or booking rides on behalf of users. This agentic feature works in the background with select apps including Uber and Grubhub, while allowing users to supervise or interrupt its work at any time. The feature is now rolling out to Pixel 10, Pixel 10 Pro, and Pixel 10 Pro XL devices.
