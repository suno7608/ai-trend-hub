<div class="lang-ko">

Google, Meta, TikTok 등 주요 플랫폼이 오디언스 타겟팅을 자동화함에 따라, 광고 크리에이티브(헤드라인, 이미지, 영상)가 누가 광고를 보는지를 결정하는 핵심 신호로 부상하고 있다. 즉, 크리에이티브 자체가 타겟팅의 역할을 수행하는 패러다임 전환이 일어나고 있다. 마케터들은 크리에이티브 전략을 타겟팅 전략과 동일한 비중으로 다루어야 한다.

</div>
<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

As Google, Meta, and TikTok increasingly automate audience targeting through AI, the creative elements of ads—headlines, images, and videos—are becoming the primary signals that determine who sees each advertisement. This represents a fundamental shift in digital advertising, where creative strategy effectively functions as targeting strategy. Marketers who invest in high-quality, well-crafted creative will have a significant advantage in AI-optimized ad environments.

</div>

## Key Points

- Google, Meta, and TikTok are automating audience targeting, reducing marketer control over who sees ads
- Ad creative (headlines, images, videos) is now the strongest signal for AI-driven audience matching
- Creative strategy and targeting strategy are converging into a single discipline
