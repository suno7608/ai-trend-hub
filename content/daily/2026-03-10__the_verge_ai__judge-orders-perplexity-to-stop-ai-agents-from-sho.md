<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

A federal judge has ordered Perplexity to stop its AI agents from shopping on Amazon on behalf of users. Amazon sued Perplexity in November, alleging unauthorized access to user accounts through the AI startup's shopping features. The court found Amazon provided strong evidence that Perplexity's Comet browser accesses user accounts without proper authorization.
