DTC cookware brand Caraway has debuted its products in over 500 Walmart stores as part of a new wholesale partnership. The move aligns with Walmart's broader strategy to elevate and diversify its product assortment with premium brands. This partnership exemplifies a growing trend of digitally native DTC brands expanding into brick-and-mortar retail through major retailers.
