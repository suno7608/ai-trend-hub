<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

ServiceNow has invested $40 million in Indian banking software specialist BusinessNext, valuing the firm at $700 million. The strategic investment aims to expand AI-powered banking software globally, with ServiceNow serving as a key growth partner. This move reflects ServiceNow's broader push to deepen its presence in the financial services vertical.
