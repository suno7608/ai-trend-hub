<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

Microsoft announced that its Gaming Copilot AI assistant will launch on current-generation Xbox consoles this year. The AI assistant is currently in beta testing on Xbox mobile app, Windows 11, and Xbox Ally handhelds, allowing players to use voice commands for help when stuck in games. Xbox plans to expand the assistant to more gaming services in the future.
