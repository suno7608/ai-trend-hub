<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

Google and generative AI platforms increasingly surface search-result snippets and direct answers, making meta descriptions a continued point of influence on user behavior. Despite AI-driven changes in search, meta descriptions remain relevant for click-through rates and brand visibility in ecommerce. Retailers should revisit their meta description strategies to stay competitive in an AI-augmented search environment.

- 메타 디스크립션은 AI 검색 환경에서도 사용자 클릭 결정에 영향을 미치는 요소로 유효함
- 이커머스 사업자는 AI 검색 최적화를 위한 메타 디스크립션 전략 업데이트가 필요함
