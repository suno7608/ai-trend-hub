Google Analytics 4 (GA4) has introduced automatic tracking of traffic sent to websites by AI chatbots. This enables marketers to more easily understand the impact AI assistants have on website visits. Accurate measurement of AI-driven traffic sources is expected to improve the precision of marketing ROI analysis.
