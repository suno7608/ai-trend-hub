<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

GameStop announced on July 15 that it has joined the Uber Eats marketplace, making video games, consoles, accessories, and collectibles available for delivery across U.S. store locations. Consumers can choose between scheduled or on-demand delivery through the platform. This partnership represents a brick-and-mortar retailer leveraging a third-party delivery marketplace to extend its omnichannel reach.

</div>

## Key Points

- GameStop is now available on Uber Eats across U.S. store locations as of July 15, 2026
- Consumers can opt for on-demand or scheduled delivery of games, consoles, accessories, and collectibles
- This signals a growing trend of traditional retailers integrating with instant-delivery platforms to drive incremental sales
