<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

Target is expanding its invite-only Target Plus marketplace by adding brands such as Forever 21 and Clarks, along with beauty labels targeting trending categories like K-beauty. The curated marketplace model allows Target to differentiate from open platforms like Amazon by maintaining quality control over its seller ecosystem. This move signals Target's intent to become a more competitive third-party commerce destination while staying on-trend with consumer demand.

- New additions include Forever 21, Clarks, and K-beauty brands targeting trending consumer categories
- Strategy positions Target as a differentiated marketplace competitor against Amazon's open platform model
