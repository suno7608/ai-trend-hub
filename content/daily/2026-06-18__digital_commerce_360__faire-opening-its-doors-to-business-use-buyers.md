B2B 도매 마켓플레이스 Faire가 재판매 목적이 아닌 자체 업무용 도매 구매자들에게도 플랫폼을 개방한다고 발표했다. 수만 개의 기업들이 수년간 자체 운영용 도매 제품 구매를 시도해 왔다는 점에서 실질적인 수요가 확인된다. 이번 비즈니스 모델 확장은 Faire의 플랫폼 성장을 위한 새로운 구매자 세그먼트 확보 전략이다.

</div>
<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

B2B wholesale marketplace Faire announced it is expanding access to business-use buyers who want to purchase wholesale products for their own operations rather than for resale. The company noted that tens of thousands of businesses have attempted to use the platform for operational purchasing over the years, validating genuine demand. This model expansion represents a strategic move to capture a new buyer segment and broaden the platform's total addressable market.

</div>

## Key Points

- Faire is opening its wholesale marketplace to business-use buyers purchasing for their own operations, not just resellers
- Tens of thousands of businesses had already attempted to use Faire for operational purchasing, confirming strong latent demand
- The expansion diversifies Faire's business model beyond its core reseller-focused marketplace
