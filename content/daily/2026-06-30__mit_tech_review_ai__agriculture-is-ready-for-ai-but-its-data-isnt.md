AI holds significant promise for agriculture, particularly in addressing volatile fertilizer costs, unpredictable weather, and razor-thin margins. However, industry leaders are cautioned against investing in AI before establishing proper data foundations. Research indicates AI-enabled predictive models can improve crop outcomes, but data readiness is a prerequisite for success.
