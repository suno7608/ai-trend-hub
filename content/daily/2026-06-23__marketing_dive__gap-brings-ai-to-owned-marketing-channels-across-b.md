<div class="lang-ko">

Gap Inc.는 브랜드 포트폴리오 전반의 자사 마케팅 채널에 AI를 도입하는 전환 프로젝트를 발표했다. Google Cloud, Zeta Global, Publicis Sapient과의 파트너십이 이번 이니셔티브의 핵심 기반이 된다. 이는 퍼스트파티 데이터 활용과 개인화 마케팅 역량을 AI로 강화하려는 전략적 행보다.

</div>
<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

Gap Inc. is bringing AI to its owned marketing channels across its entire brand portfolio as part of a newly announced transformation effort. The company is partnering with Google Cloud, Zeta Global, and Publicis Sapient to power this initiative. The move reflects a strategic push to enhance personalization and first-party data capabilities through AI.

</div>

## Key Points

- Gap is applying AI to owned marketing channels (email, app, web) across all its brands
- Three strategic partners — Google Cloud, Zeta Global, Publicis Sapient — underpin the transformation
- The focus on owned channels reflects a broader industry shift toward first-party data and reduced reliance on paid media
