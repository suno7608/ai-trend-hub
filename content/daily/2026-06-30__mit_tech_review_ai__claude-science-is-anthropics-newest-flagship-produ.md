<div class="lang-ko">

Anthropic이 제약사 임원, 바이오텍 창업자, 연구자 대상 이벤트에서 Claude Science를 공개했다. Claude Code가 소프트웨어 엔지니어링을 지원하듯, Claude Science는 과학 연구를 자율적으로 지원하는 것을 목표로 하며 간결한 고수준 지시만으로 의미 있는 작업을 수행할 수 있다. 이 제품은 생명과학 분야 AI 에이전트의 실용적 적용 범위를 크게 확장하는 신호탄으로 평가된다.

</div>
<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

Anthropic announced Claude Science at an event for pharmaceutical executives, biotech founders, and researchers, positioning it as a flagship product for supporting scientific research. Similar to Claude Code's role in software engineering, Claude Science can autonomously execute meaningful research tasks from concise, high-level instructions. The product signals a significant expansion of autonomous AI agent capabilities into the life sciences domain.

</div>

## Key Points

- Anthropic launched Claude Science, an autonomous AI agent designed to support scientific research with minimal human instruction
- Claude Science mirrors the Claude Code model, applying autonomous task execution to the life sciences domain
- The product was unveiled at a targeted event for pharmaceutical and biotech leaders, signaling a focused enterprise go-to-market strategy
