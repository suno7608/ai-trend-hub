<div class="lang-ko">

AI 검색 최적화(AI Search Optimization)는 ChatGPT, Gemini, AI Overviews 같은 AI 답변 엔진에서 브랜드가 인용·언급될 확률을 높이는 마케팅 실천법이다. AI 도구가 유입한 방문자는 일반 검색 방문자 대비 약 11배 높은 회원가입률을 보인다는 Microsoft Clarity 연구 결과가 있어 트래픽 규모는 작지만 구매 의도가 매우 높다. 이는 기존 SEO 전략을 AI 중심으로 재편해야 할 필요성을 강력히 시사한다.

</div>
<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

AI search optimization is the emerging practice of improving a brand's chances of being cited by AI answer engines such as ChatGPT, Gemini, and AI Overviews, replacing traditional keyword-based SEO logic. According to a Microsoft Clarity study across 1,200+ publisher and news sites, visitors referred by AI tools converted to sign-ups at roughly 11 times the rate of traditional search visitors, indicating extremely high purchase intent despite lower traffic volumes. Marketers are urged to adapt their content and discoverability strategies to align with how AI models surface and recommend brands.

</div>

## Key Points

- AI search optimization focuses on getting brands cited by AI answer engines like ChatGPT, Gemini, and AI Overviews
- Microsoft Clarity study: AI-referred visitors convert to sign-ups at ~11x the rate of traditional search visitors across 1,200+ sites
- Traffic volume from AI referrals is currently small but carries significantly higher purchase intent
- Marketers must rethink content authority and structure to be surfaced by AI recommendation engines
