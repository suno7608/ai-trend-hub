<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

According to McKinsey, 50% of consumers now use AI-powered search, with over 70% relying on it to gather information, signaling a fundamental shift in search behavior. SEO leaders must now adopt Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) strategies to secure brand visibility within AI-generated answers on platforms like Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, and Perplexity. HubSpot's analysis compares Profound and Peec AI as two emerging tools designed to support AEO workflows.

</div>

## Key Points

- 50% of consumers use AI-powered search per McKinsey; 70%+ use it to gather information — a critical shift for brand discoverability
- AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) is emerging as a mandatory complement to traditional SEO for visibility in AI-generated answers
- Profound and Peec AI are purpose-built tools to help brands optimize for AI search platforms including ChatGPT and Perplexity
