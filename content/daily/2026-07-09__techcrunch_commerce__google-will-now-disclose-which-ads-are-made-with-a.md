<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

Google is expanding its AI content disclosure requirement, which previously applied only to election ads, to cover all advertisements featuring synthetic or digitally altered content. While Google already prohibits misleading and deceptive ads, the disclosure of AI-generated creative elements was not broadly mandated until now. This policy shift represents a significant step toward greater transparency standards for AI-generated advertising across the industry.
