<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

Seven major tech companies including Google, Meta, and Microsoft signed a 'rate payer protection pledge' with President Trump to cover electricity costs for AI data centers. This addresses bipartisan concerns about rising electricity rates due to energy-hungry data centers. Trump stated this would help reduce community opposition to data center construction.
