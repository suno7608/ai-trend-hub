<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

Instacart has acquired Arpalus, a computer vision company specializing in real-time grocery shelf intelligence. Arpalus technology converts video footage of store shelves into product availability data that can be used to improve online order accuracy. The acquisition is aimed at directly addressing undetected out-of-stock issues that impact the online grocery shopping experience.

</div>

## Key Points

- Instacart acquires Arpalus to enhance AI-driven grocery shelf monitoring capabilities
- Arpalus converts store shelf video into real-time product availability data for online shoppers
- The deal targets the persistent problem of undetected out-of-stock items in online grocery orders
- Acquisition signals Instacart's strategic push to deepen AI integration across the retail supply chain
