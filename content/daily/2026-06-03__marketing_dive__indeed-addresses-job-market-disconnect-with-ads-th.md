Indeed launched the 'Jobs Need People' campaign emphasizing human connections in the workplace. The campaign depicts peer-to-peer connections that make work fulfilling amid rising trends like employer ghosting. It addresses job market disconnects by putting human-centered values at the core of its marketing message.
