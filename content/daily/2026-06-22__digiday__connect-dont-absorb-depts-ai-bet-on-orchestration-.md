<div class="lang-ko">

디지털 에이전시 Dept는 자체 AI 어시스턴트가 이미 전체 매출의 약 20%에 관여하고 있으며, 내년까지 이를 80%로 확대할 계획이다. 핵심 전략은 단일 AI 운영체제를 구축하는 대신 기존 도구들을 연결하는 '오케스트레이션' 방식이다. 이는 AI를 흡수·통합하는 방식이 아니라 연결하는 접근법으로, 에이전시 업계의 AI 전환 방향을 제시한다.

</div>
<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

Digital agency Dept reports its AI assistant already influences roughly one-fifth of its total revenue, with an ambitious target to reach 80% by next year. Rather than building a monolithic AI operating system, Dept is betting on an orchestration strategy that connects existing tools and workflows. This 'connect, don't absorb' philosophy positions Dept as a case study in pragmatic, scalable AI integration for marketing services.

</div>

## Key Points

- Dept's AI assistant already touches ~20% of revenue with a target of 80% by next year
- Strategy is orchestration (connecting tools) rather than building a single AI OS
- 'Connect, don't absorb' philosophy reduces integration risk and accelerates adoption
- Signals a broader agency industry shift toward AI-native operating models
