<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

ChatGPT is now accessible through Apple CarPlay with iOS 26.4 update and the latest ChatGPT app version. Apple added support for voice-based conversational apps in CarPlay, enabling AI chatbot usage while driving. The CarPlay integration supports only voice conversations without displaying text or image responses.
