<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

Inaccurate address data begins at checkout and leads to delivery issues, checkout friction, and fraud risk. Retailers continue investing in faster delivery and expanded fulfillment networks. Improving address data quality at the point of entry is crucial for operational efficiency.

</div>

## Key Points

- Bad address data creates cascade of fulfillment problems
- Data quality issues start at the checkout process
- Address verification at entry point prevents downstream issues
