<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

Spangler Candy, owner of the Dum Dums brand, has carved out a strong niche in the candy market by centering its strategy on nostalgia and heritage brand storytelling. VP of Marketing Evan Brock attributed the company's success to its portfolio of brands with storied histories that resonate emotionally with consumers. The approach demonstrates how legacy brands can leverage nostalgia as a sustainable competitive differentiator.
