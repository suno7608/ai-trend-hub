<div class="lang-ko">

AI를 활용한 콘텐츠 대량 생성은 콘텐츠 획일화라는 심각한 위험을 초래할 수 있다. 주요 플랫폼들은 AI가 생산성을 높이는 도구이지만, 진정한 창의성은 여전히 인간에게서 나온다는 데 동의하고 있다. 브랜드와 플랫폼 모두 규모 확장과 창의적 독창성 사이의 균형을 찾는 것이 핵심 과제로 부상하고 있다.

</div>
<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

The widespread use of AI for content creation risks producing homogeneous content that fails to differentiate brands. Major platforms acknowledge this tension but maintain that genuine creativity will always originate from humans. Balancing AI-driven scale with creative uniqueness has emerged as a defining challenge for brands and platforms alike.

</div>

## Key Points

- AI-generated content at scale risks creating homogeneous, undifferentiated brand messaging
- Major platforms agree that human creativity remains essential even as AI handles execution
- Brands must actively strategize to maintain creative distinctiveness in an AI-saturated content environment
