우버 이츠가 마켓플레이스에 다수의 신규 소매업체를 추가하며 라스트마일 배송 서비스를 확대했다. 신규 파트너에는 Digital Commerce 360 Top 2000 및 Europe Database에 등재된 소매업체와 FedEx Office가 포함된다. 이번 확장은 우버 이츠가 음식 배달을 넘어 종합 소매 배송 플랫폼으로 진화하는 전략의 일환이다.

<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

Uber Eats has announced the addition of several new retailers to its marketplace to enable third-party last-mile delivery from their physical stores. New partners include retailers from the Digital Commerce 360 Top 2000 and Europe Database, as well as FedEx Office. This expansion signals Uber Eats' continued push beyond food delivery into broader retail logistics.

## Key Points

- Uber Eats is expanding its marketplace to include general retailers, not just food vendors
- FedEx Office is among the new retail partners, blending logistics and retail
- Third-party last-mile delivery from stores is becoming a key competitive differentiator
