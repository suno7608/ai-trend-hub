<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

According to Digiday research, marketers acknowledge AI's benefits for improving marketing workflows, but trust and complexity issues act as adoption barriers. Marketers recognize the benefits of AI tools while expressing concerns about technology reliability and usage complexity. This has emerged as a major factor hindering widespread adoption of AI marketing technologies.
