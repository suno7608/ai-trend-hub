<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

Salesforce has published a framework outlining five requirements for establishing trust in agent-to-agent (A2A) communication. The framework goes beyond individual AI agent capabilities to address interoperability and governance in multi-agent environments. Secure and trusted A2A communication is positioned as a foundational requirement for the next wave of AI commerce and enterprise automation.
