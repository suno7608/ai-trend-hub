<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

Tractor Supply is leveraging real customers in its advertising to differentiate from AI-generated content that consumers are increasingly wary of. The company is focusing on authentic customer stories, local events, and multi-channel marketing to drive spring traffic to stores. This strategy represents a counter-movement to the proliferation of AI-generated marketing content in the retail space.
