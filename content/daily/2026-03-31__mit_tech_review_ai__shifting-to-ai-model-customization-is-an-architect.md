대규모 언어모델(LLM)의 성능 향상이 과거 10배 급성장에서 점진적 개선으로 변화했습니다. 현재는 도메인 특화 인텔리전스에서만 진정한 단계적 향상이 나타나고 있습니다. 조직의 데이터와 모델을 융합한 맞춤형 AI가 핵심 경쟁력이 되고 있습니다.

</div>
<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

Large language models have shifted from massive 10x performance jumps to incremental gains between iterations. Domain-specialized intelligence remains the exception where true step-function improvements still occur. Organizations are finding competitive advantage by fusing AI models with their specific data and requirements.

</div>

## Key Points

- LLM performance improvements have plateaued from exponential to incremental gains
- Domain-specialized AI still shows significant step-function improvements
- Custom model integration with organizational data creates competitive advantage
