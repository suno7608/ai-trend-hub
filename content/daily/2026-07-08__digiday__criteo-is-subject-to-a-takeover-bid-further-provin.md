Criteo, a major ad tech company, has received a takeover bid from Vista Equity and Quinti Capital at a 50% premium over its stock price. The bid underscores private equity's sustained interest in ad tech, raising questions about where these firms see long-term value in the sector. Industry observers suggest PE firms are attracted to Criteo's performance marketing data assets and retargeting technology.
