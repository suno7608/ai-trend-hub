Alibaba has reportedly classified Anthropic's Claude Code as high-risk software and banned its employees from using it. This aligns with a broader trend of Chinese tech firms tightening restrictions on foreign AI tools due to data security and sovereignty concerns. The move underscores how geopolitical dynamics are increasingly shaping enterprise AI adoption policies.
