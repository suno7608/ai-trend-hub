<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

Unilever has deployed AI to vet creators and automate operational workflows across its 300,000-creator influencer network, one of the largest in the industry. Critically, the company retains human judgment for creative decisions and relationship management, using AI to handle scale while preserving authenticity. This hybrid model represents a benchmark approach for managing creator ecosystems at enterprise scale.
