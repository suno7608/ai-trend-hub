J. Crew has hired Stacey Levitt, a former Walmart e-commerce executive with nearly a decade of experience at the big-box retailer, as its new Executive Vice President of E-Commerce and Digital Experience. The appointment signals J. Crew's strategic intent to strengthen its digital commerce capabilities and enhance the online customer experience. This leadership hire reflects a broader industry trend of fashion and apparel brands investing in seasoned digital commerce talent to compete in an increasingly online retail environment.
