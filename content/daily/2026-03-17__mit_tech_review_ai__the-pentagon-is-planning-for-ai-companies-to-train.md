<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

According to MIT Technology Review, the Pentagon is planning secure environments for generative AI companies to train military-specific models on classified data. AI models like Anthropic's Claude are already used in classified settings including analyzing targets in Iran. The Pentagon is discussing new approaches to allow models to train on classified information.
