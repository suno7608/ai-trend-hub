구글이 미국 내 모든 사용자에게 개인화된 제미나이 AI의 Personal Intelligence 기능을 확대 제공한다고 발표했습니다. 이전에는 Google AI Pro 및 Ultra 구독자에게만 제한되었던 기능입니다. 이 기능은 YouTube, Google Photos, Gmail 등 연결된 앱의 데이터를 활용해 제미나이의 응답과 제안에 맥락을 제공합니다.

</div>
<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

Google announced that all US users will now have access to Gemini AI's Personal Intelligence feature, which was previously limited to AI Pro and Ultra subscribers. The feature uses data from connected apps like YouTube, Google Photos, and Gmail to provide context for Gemini's responses and suggestions. It's available through AI Mode in Search, Gemini in Chrome, and the Gemini app for personal accounts only.

</div>

## Key Points

- Personal Intelligence feature expanded to all US users from premium-only access
- Integrates data from multiple Google apps for contextual AI responses
- Currently limited to personal accounts, not business or enterprise users
