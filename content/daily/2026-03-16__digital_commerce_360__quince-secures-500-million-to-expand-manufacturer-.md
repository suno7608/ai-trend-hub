Online retailer Quince raised $500 million in funding, valuing the company at $10.1 billion. Founded in 2018 in San Francisco, the company sells apparel, accessories, and home goods directly from manufacturers to consumers. The company focuses on 'affordable luxury' products, including cashmere items.
