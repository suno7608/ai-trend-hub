<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

Google Research published work on using generative AI to quantify uncertainty in weather forecasting. They developed innovative weather prediction models like MetNet-3 and GraphCast to provide accurate and timely weather information. Weather is inherently stochastic, and they're improving upon traditional physics-based simulation methods.
