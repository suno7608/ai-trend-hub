수년간 AI 칩과 컴퓨팅 파워 확보에 집중해온 기업들이 이제는 인프라를 효과적으로 활용하는 것이 더 어려운 과제임을 깨닫고 있다. 컴퓨팅 자원에 대한 접근 자체보다 이를 최대한 활용하는 역량이 AI 경쟁력의 새로운 핵심이 되고 있다. 기업 리더들은 컴퓨팅 효율성과 최적화를 다음 단계 AI 전략의 중심에 놓고 있다.

</div>
<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

After years focused on securing AI chips and computing infrastructure, enterprise leaders are now finding that effectively utilizing that compute is the more difficult and critical challenge. Access to AI infrastructure is becoming commoditized, shifting the competitive advantage toward how efficiently organizations can leverage their compute resources. Enterprise AI strategy is pivoting from infrastructure acquisition to optimization and effective deployment.

</div>

## Key Points

- Securing AI compute is no longer the primary bottleneck for enterprises
- Effectively utilizing computing infrastructure is emerging as the new competitive differentiator
- Enterprise AI strategy must evolve from acquisition to optimization and deployment effectiveness
