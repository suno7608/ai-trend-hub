Google and Samsung launched Gemini's task automation feature in beta on their newest devices. Starting with food delivery and rideshare apps, Gemini can use apps on your behalf in a virtual window to handle tasks like ordering dinner or booking airport rides. The AI assistant performs actual app operations based on simple prompts, creating an impressive automated experience.
