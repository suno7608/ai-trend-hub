Genuine Parts Co. plans to separate its automotive and industrial operations into two publicly traded companies. The tax-free separation, expected to complete in Q1 2027, will sharpen B2B ecommerce focus across both sectors. This will create an independent Global Automotive company and a standalone industrial company.
