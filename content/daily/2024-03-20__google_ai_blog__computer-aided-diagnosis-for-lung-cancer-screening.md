Google developed an AI-powered computer-aided diagnosis system for lung cancer screening using CT scans. The technology can reduce mortality by at least 20% in high-risk populations through early detection. The US Preventive Services Task Force recently expanded screening recommendations by 80%, increasing access for women and minority groups.
