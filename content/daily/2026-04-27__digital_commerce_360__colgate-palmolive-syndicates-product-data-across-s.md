<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

Colgate-Palmolive began syndicating product-related content across multiple retailers' ecommerce websites. This effort aims to standardize what shoppers see when making purchase decisions. The company wanted to avoid manually distributing content to individual retailers and instead distribute product content systematically.
