Netherlands-based grocery conglomerate Ahold Delhaize has appointed former Amazon executive Claire Peters as the new CEO of its USA operations. Peters is set to assume the role on September 8 and will oversee all of the company's U.S. retail banners. The appointment of an Amazon alumna signals Ahold Delhaize's intent to strengthen its digital commerce and data capabilities.
