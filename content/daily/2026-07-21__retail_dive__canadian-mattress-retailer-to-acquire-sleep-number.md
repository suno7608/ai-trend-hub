<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

Canadian mattress retailer Sleep Country has agreed to acquire U.S. mattress maker and retailer Sleep Number for over $700 million, with the deal having been arranged prior to Sleep Number's bankruptcy filing. Sleep Number, known for its smart adjustable mattresses, entered bankruptcy amid financial pressures in the competitive sleep products market. The acquisition highlights consolidation trends in the North American mattress retail sector and the financial vulnerabilities faced by some D2C-oriented smart home brands.
