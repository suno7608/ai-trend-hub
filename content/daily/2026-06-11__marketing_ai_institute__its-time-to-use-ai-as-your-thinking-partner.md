<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

Most marketers maintain a transactional relationship with AI, according to Content Monsta founder A. Lee Judge. They make requests, receive assets, edit them to match their voice, and repeat this cycle. Judge emphasizes that AI isn't meant to replace human content creators but to elevate them as thinking partners.

</div>

## Key Points

- Most marketers use AI transactionally rather than strategically
- AI should serve as a thinking partner to elevate human creativity
- Moving beyond simple request-response cycles improves content quality
