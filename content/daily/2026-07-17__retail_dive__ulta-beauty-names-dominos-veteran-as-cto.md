<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

Ulta Beauty has named Kelly Garcia, a tech veteran from Domino's, as its new Chief Technology Officer to spearhead a technology-driven growth and personalization strategy. Garcia has served on Ulta's board since 2022 and now steps into an executive role at a pivotal time for the retailer. The appointment underscores the growing importance of technology and AI-driven personalization as competitive differentiators in retail.
