<div class="lang-ko">

대다수 소매업체가 연간 기술 투자에 5천만 달러 이상을 지출하고 있으며, 그 중 28%는 1억~2억5천만 달러를 투자하고 있다. 올해 주요 투자 우선순위는 AI와 사이버보안으로, 디지털 전환 가속화가 전략적 핵심으로 부상하고 있다. 이는 소매 산업 전반에서 AI 인프라 경쟁이 심화되고 있음을 시사한다.

</div>
<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

The majority of retailers allocate more than $50 million annually to technology investments, with 28% spending between $100 million and $250 million per year. AI and cybersecurity have emerged as the top investment priorities for the current year, reflecting accelerating digital transformation across retail. This signals intensifying infrastructure competition as retailers race to build AI-ready operations.

</div>

## Key Points

- Majority of retailers spend over $50M annually on technology; 28% spend $100M–$250M
- AI and cybersecurity are the top investment priorities for retailers in the current year
- The scale of investment signals that AI infrastructure is becoming a baseline competitive requirement, not a differentiator
