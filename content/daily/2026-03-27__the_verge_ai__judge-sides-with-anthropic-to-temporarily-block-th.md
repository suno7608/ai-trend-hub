<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

AI company Anthropic sued the Pentagon over its designation as a supply chain risk. A federal judge granted Anthropic a preliminary injunction to temporarily block the government blacklisting. The judge ruled that the Department of Defense's designation based on Anthropic's 'hostile manner through the press' violated First Amendment rights.
