Palo Alto-based startup Axiom Math has released Axplorer, a free AI tool designed to help mathematicians discover mathematical patterns. The tool aims to unlock solutions to long-standing mathematical problems. It represents a redesign of the existing PatternBoost tool, targeting innovation in mathematical research methods.
