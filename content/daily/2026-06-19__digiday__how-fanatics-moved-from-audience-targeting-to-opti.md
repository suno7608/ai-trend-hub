<div class="lang-ko">

스포츠 커머스 기업 Fanatics는 기존 오디언스 타겟팅 중심의 캠페인 전략에서 고객 생애 가치(LTV) 최적화 방식으로 전환하며 LTV에서 19% 향상을 달성했다. 이 전환은 결과 기반 미디어 바잉(outcome-based media buying)에 대한 브랜드들의 관심 증가를 반영한다. Fanatics의 사례는 단기 전환보다 장기 고객 가치를 극대화하는 마케팅 패러다임 변화를 보여준다.

</div>
<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

Fanatics has transitioned its marketing strategy from traditional audience targeting to optimizing campaigns around customer lifetime value (LTV), resulting in a reported 19% lift in LTV. This shift reflects a broader industry move toward outcome-based media buying focused on long-term customer relationships. The case highlights how data-driven LTV optimization is becoming a competitive differentiator in sports commerce.

</div>

## Key Points

- Fanatics achieved a 19% lift in customer LTV after moving from audience targeting to LTV-optimized campaigns
- Outcome-based media buying is emerging as a key strategy for brands prioritizing long-term customer relationships
- The shift signals a broader marketing industry move away from short-term conversion metrics toward sustainable revenue growth
