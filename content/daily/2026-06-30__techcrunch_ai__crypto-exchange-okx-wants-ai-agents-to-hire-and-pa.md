Crypto exchange OKX is developing a marketplace that enables AI agents to autonomously hire and pay one another, integrating payments, identity, and reputation into a unified agentic ecosystem. The platform envisions a new form of agentic commerce where AI agents transact and collaborate independently. Blockchain-based identity and reputation systems are positioned as the trust infrastructure underpinning agent-to-agent interactions.
