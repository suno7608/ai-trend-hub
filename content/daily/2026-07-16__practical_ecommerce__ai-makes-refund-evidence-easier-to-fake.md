<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

Advances in AI are enabling fraudsters to easily generate fabricated product images and manipulate refund evidence for ecommerce scams. Scammers can now adjust their explanations and even automate the entire process to repeat fraud at scale. This emerging threat puts pressure on online retailers to develop more robust AI-driven fraud detection and verification systems.
