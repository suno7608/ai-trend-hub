<div class="lang-ko">

셔윈-윌리엄스는 B2B 결제 생태계를 강화하기 위해 Square와 파트너십을 맺고, 전문 페인터·시공업자·디자이너 등 Pro+ 고객 대상 디지털 경험에 결제, 인보이싱, 현금 흐름 관리 기능을 직접 통합한다. 이는 단순 제품 공급자를 넘어 고객의 비즈니스 운영 파트너로 역할을 확장하려는 전략이다. B2B 고객 락인(lock-in)을 강화하고 플랫폼 의존도를 높이는 효과가 기대된다.

</div>
<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

Sherwin-Williams has selected Square to embed payment, invoicing, and cash flow management tools directly into its Digital Alliance Program for Pro+ customers, which includes professional painters, contractors, and designers. This integration transforms Sherwin-Williams from a product supplier into a deeper operational partner embedded in its B2B customers' business workflows. The move is designed to increase customer stickiness and platform dependency among professional buyers.

</div>

## Key Points

- Sherwin-Williams integrates Square's payment and invoicing tools into its Pro+ customer digital platform
- Target customers include professional painters, contractors, and designers within the Digital Alliance Program
- Strategy deepens B2B customer lock-in by becoming embedded in daily business operations
