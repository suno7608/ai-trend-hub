Carrier Enterprise(CE)는 Watsco Inc.와 Carrier Corporation의 합작 법인으로, 현재 전체 매출의 60%를 디지털 채널을 통해 창출하고 있다. CE는 HVAC 시스템 유통업체로서 디지털 전환을 통해 주요 수익원을 온라인으로 전환하는 데 성공했다. 이 성과는 B2B 산업재 분야에서도 디지털 커머스가 핵심 판매 채널로 자리잡을 수 있음을 보여준다.

</div>
<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

Carrier Enterprise (CE), the joint venture between Watsco Inc. and Carrier Corporation, now attributes 60% of its total revenue to digital channels. The HVAC distributor shared new details on how digital sales have become central to its business model. This milestone demonstrates that even industrial B2B sectors can achieve dominant digital channel share.

</div>

## Key Points

- Carrier Enterprise now generates 60% of total revenue through digital channels
- CE operates as a distributor for HVAC systems under the Watsco-Carrier joint venture
- The achievement signals that industrial and B2B sectors can lead in digital commerce adoption
- Digital channel investment is becoming a critical competitive differentiator in distribution
