Nvidia unveiled DLSS 5, described as the company's most significant computer graphics breakthrough since real-time ray tracing in 2018. The technology claims to infuse pixels with photoreal lighting and materials, but actually transforms recognizable faces into AI-generated-looking appearances. Characters from Resident Evil and Hogwarts Legacy appeared unnaturally altered, drawing criticism.
