<div class="lang-ko">

AI 검색 시대에 브랜드 가시성을 측정하기 위해 기존 SERP 순위 외에 AI 검색 내 언급 횟수, 인용, 점유율(share of voice) 등 새로운 지표가 필요하다. HubSpot은 '답변 엔진 최적화(AEO)'라는 개념을 중심으로 브랜드가 AI 검색에서 어떻게 추적되는지 안내한다. 마케터와 SEO 담당자들은 기존 검색 전략에 더해 AI 검색 최적화 전략을 병행해야 한다.

</div>
<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

In the age of answer engine optimization (AEO), traditional SERP rankings no longer tell the full story of brand visibility in search. Marketers must now track new metrics such as AI search mentions, citations, and share of voice to accurately gauge their brand's presence. HubSpot outlines how to adapt SEO strategies to account for AI-driven search engines that synthesize answers rather than just list links.

</div>

## Key Points

- Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is emerging as a critical discipline alongside traditional SEO
- Key new metrics include AI search mentions, citations, and share of voice
- Brand visibility in AI search requires different tracking tools and strategies than conventional SERP monitoring
