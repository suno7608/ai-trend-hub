<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

Salesforce's Agentforce blog clarifies the distinction between email delivery—whether an email successfully reaches a recipient's mail server—and email deliverability, which refers to whether the email actually lands in the inbox. Understanding both concepts is critical for marketers aiming to maximize campaign effectiveness. The post also addresses recent changes in the email landscape that affect both metrics.

</div>

## Key Points

- Email delivery and email deliverability are distinct concepts that are often conflated by marketers
- Deliverability—whether emails reach the inbox—is more strategically important than basic delivery success
- Recent changes in the email ecosystem make understanding these distinctions increasingly critical for campaign performance
