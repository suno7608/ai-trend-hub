<div class="lang-ko">

Bose는 새로운 엔터테인먼트 사업부를 설립하며 브랜드 콘텐츠 전략을 내재화하고 있다. CMO는 5년간 외부 크리에이티브 에이전시를 사용하지 않았으며, 이 두 가지 움직임은 긴밀히 연관되어 있다. 이는 Bose가 직접 콘텐츠를 생산·유통하는 미디어 기업으로 진화하고 있음을 시사한다.

</div>
<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

Bose has launched a new entertainment division as part of a broader strategy to internalize its creative and content capabilities. The company's CMO has not worked with a traditional creative agency in five years, signaling a full commitment to in-house production. This move positions Bose as a brand-as-media-company, directly owning the content and entertainment experience it delivers to consumers.

</div>

## Key Points

- Bose established a dedicated entertainment division to own content creation end-to-end
- CMO has operated without a traditional creative agency for five years, demonstrating viability of full in-house model
- The strategy reflects a broader trend of brands becoming entertainment and media companies
