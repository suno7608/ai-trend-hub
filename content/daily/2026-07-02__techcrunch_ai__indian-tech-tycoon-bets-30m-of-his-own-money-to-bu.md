<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

Indian tech entrepreneur Bhavin Turakhia has personally invested $30 million to build Neo, an AI-native enterprise software suite designed to compete with Microsoft Office and Google Workspace. Neo represents Turakhia's fifth venture and is focused on reimagining productivity software with AI at its core. The move signals a growing wave of AI-first challengers targeting entrenched enterprise software incumbents.
