<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

An investigation revealed that Meta's AI smart glasses are reportedly sending sensitive footage to human reviewers in Kenya. According to Swedish media reports, contractors in Kenya have reviewed videos showing bathroom visits, intimate moments, and other private activities. This has led to a class action lawsuit alleging violations of privacy laws and false advertising.
