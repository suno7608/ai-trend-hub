According to Algolia's 2026 B2B Ecommerce Site Search Trends Report, 83% of B2B sellers now prioritize AI when selecting search tools. The report is based on a survey of 300 senior business and technology decision-makers across North America. AI-powered search and discovery have evolved from emerging capability to core infrastructure in B2B ecommerce.
