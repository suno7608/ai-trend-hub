<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

Walmart launched its Upstream Facility Services portal to manage facility operations including HVAC, plumbing, and maintenance for business clients. The portal is based on the internal services system used in Walmart and Sam's Club locations across the U.S. Walmart is targeting commercial clients to expand its B2B services business.
