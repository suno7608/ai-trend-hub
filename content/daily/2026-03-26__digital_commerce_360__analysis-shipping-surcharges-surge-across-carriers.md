Shipping costs are rising again as UPS and FedEx rely more heavily on surcharges rather than base rate increases to offset higher fuel, labor, and network costs. This approach differs from how many B2B ecommerce operators had modeled shipping cost structures. The shift represents a strategic change in how carriers manage pricing pressures.
