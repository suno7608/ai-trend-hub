<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

Nvidia announced a $4 billion investment in photonics technology companies to advance AI data center capabilities. The company is investing $2 billion each in Lumentum and Coherent to develop optical transceivers, circuit switches, and lasers. These technologies are expected to significantly improve data transfer speeds, energy efficiency, and bandwidth in future AI data centers.
