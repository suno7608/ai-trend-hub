Shopify announced it is extending business-to-business features to its direct-to-consumer merchants. Merchants using 'basic,' 'grow,' and 'advanced' plans now have access to B2B features at no extra cost. Merchants on plans other than Shopify Plus can now utilize B2B capabilities.
