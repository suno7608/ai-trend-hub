<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

German startup TrustedCranes launched a B2B marketplace targeting the U.S. market for used mobile cranes. The platform aims to bring structure and brokerage oversight to the fragmented U.S. used crane market. It operates as a concierge-style digital marketplace for construction and industrial buyers seeking alternatives to expensive new equipment.
