OpenAI is shifting its checkout plans within its agentic commerce strategy by reportedly sidelining ChatGPT's Instant Checkout feature. The company is pivoting toward checkout experiences through merchants using ChatGPT apps instead. The Agentic Commerce Protocol (ACP) co-developed with Stripe appears set to continue.
