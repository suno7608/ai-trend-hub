<div class="lang-ko">

Blue Chip의 CMO Dan Eisenberg는 Emerald Nuts 등 챌린저 브랜드가 대기업 대비 제한된 마케팅 예산으로 차별화를 이루는 전략을 소개했다. 핵심은 광범위한 마케팅 시도를 줄이고 선택과 집중을 통해 임팩트 있는 캠페인을 실행하는 것이다. 자원이 제한된 브랜드일수록 정밀하고 일관된 브랜드 메시지 전략이 경쟁 우위의 원천이 된다.

</div>
<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

Dan Eisenberg, CMO at Chicago-based agency Blue Chip, explains how challenger brands like Emerald Nuts can differentiate themselves against corporate giants despite limited marketing budgets. The core strategy involves reducing the number of marketing attempts and focusing resources on fewer, higher-impact campaigns. For resource-constrained brands, precision and consistency in brand messaging serve as key competitive advantages.

</div>

## Key Points

- Challenger brands benefit from fewer, more focused marketing campaigns over broad, scattered efforts
- Emerald Nuts is cited as an example of a brand successfully competing against larger corporate rivals
- Precision and consistency in brand messaging are identified as core competitive tools for resource-limited brands
