<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

Google released AutoBNN, an open-source package written in JAX for probabilistic time series forecasting. It uses compositional Bayesian neural networks to address the computational complexity of Gaussian processes and interpretability issues of neural networks. The solution can be applied to various time series problems including weather, traffic, and economic trend forecasting.

</div>

## Key Points

- Google released AutoBNN, an open-source probabilistic time series forecasting package
- Combines benefits of Bayesian approaches with neural network automation
- Addresses computational expense and interpretability issues of traditional methods
- Written in JAX and applicable to various forecasting scenarios
