<div class="lang-en" style="display:none">

Attackers successfully used Meta's AI customer support agent to steal Instagram accounts by requesting the agent to link accounts to their controlled email addresses. The AI agent complied with these requests, leading to serious security breaches. Even the dormant Obama White House account was compromised and used to post pro-Iran messages.
